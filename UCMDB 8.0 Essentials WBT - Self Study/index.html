UCMDB 8.0 Essentials WBT - Self Study

Course Description  (Click to Expand)

This course is designed to teach an understanding of the fundamental concepts and applications of the HP Universal CMDB in the enterprise. Participants are introduced to the history and evolution of the UCMDB, its architecture and components, and its many strengths and features. The course aims to teach the skills behind designing and building views to model business applications and scenarios, as well as uncover business assets, infrastructure components, and the relationships existing between them. Following the introductory UCMDB topics, this course delves deeper into Modeling concepts such as TQL and Views, and the more advanced Correlation and Enrichment topics. The subject of Reports is introduced to provide an overview of the numerous reporting and data visualization and sharing options. The course wraps up by Introducing the Discovery process and providing an overview of DDM (Data and Dependency Mapping).

Intended Audience:

  • Project Managers
  • Application Modelers
  • UCMDB Implementation Consultants
